---
title: How do I verify my Tima account?
description: Steps to verify your identity on Tima.
---

## Why verify?

Account verification keeps your financial activities secure and compliant.

## Steps

1. Sign in at `https://app.tima.com`
2. Go to **Profile > Verification**
3. Upload a valid ID and a recent utility bill
4. Submit and wait for approval (can take up to 24 hours)

## Need help?
Contact `support@tima.com` if your verification is delayed.
