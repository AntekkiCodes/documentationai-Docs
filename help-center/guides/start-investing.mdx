## Start Investing

Invest with Tima in a few simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Visit `https://app.tima.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add funds">
    Go to **Wallet > Deposit** and follow instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an opportunity">
    Browse available opportunities and read details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invest">
    Enter the amount and confirm your investment. Watch for confirmation!
  </Step>
</Steps>
