---
title: Deposit Not Reflected
description: What to do if your deposit to Tima isn't showing up.
---

## Symptoms

- Deposit made, funds not showing in balance
- No confirmation email

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check processing time">
    Bank transfers may take 1-2 business days to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm transaction details">
    Make sure the reference number matches your Tima account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email `support@tima.com` with your transaction receipt if not resolved.
  </Step>
</Steps>
