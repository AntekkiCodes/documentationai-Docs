## Overview

Get started with Tima in minutes. Follow these steps to create your account, verify your identity, explore the dashboard, and make your first investment decision. Tima provides secure access to financial opportunities while ensuring regulatory compliance.

<Callout kind="info">

**Prerequisites**
- A valid government-issued ID (passport or driver's license)
- An active email address
- Access to a smartphone or computer with internet
- Be in a supported region (check [Tima's coverage](https://app.tima.com/regions))

</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Begin by registering on the Tima platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.tima.com/register` and enter your email, create a password, and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Tima. Click the confirmation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Log in and fill in your personal details including full name, address, and phone number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Next, verify your identity to unlock full features.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Verification" icon="check-circle">
    Upload a photo of your ID. Tima uses automated checks for most users, typically completing in `<5` minutes.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Advanced KYC" icon="shield">
    For higher limits, provide a selfie and proof of address. Approval takes `1-2` business days.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Make Your First Investment

Review opportunities carefully. Start small to understand the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fund Your Wallet" icon="credit-card">
    Deposit funds via bank transfer or card. Minimum: `$100`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Opportunity" icon="trending-up">
    Choose from curated options like OQtima funds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Investment" icon="check">
    Enter amount and review terms before confirming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For programmatic access, use the Tima API:

<Request tabs="JavaScript,cURL" show-lines="true">
  ````javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/investments', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}`,
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      opportunityId: 'oqtima-fund-123',
      amount: 1000,
      currency: 'USD'
    })
  });
  const data = await response.json();
  console.log(data);
  ````

  ````bash
  curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/investments \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{
      "opportunityId": "oqtima-fund-123",
      "amount": 1000,
      "currency": "USD"
    }'
  ````
</Request>

<Response tabs="200">
  ````json
  {
    "success": true,
    "investmentId": "inv_abc123",
    "status": "confirmed",
    "expectedReturn": "8-12%"
  }
  ````
</Response>

<ParamField path="opportunityId" param-type="string" required="true">
  Unique ID of the investment opportunity.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="amount" param-type="number" required="true">
  Investment amount in base currency.
</ParamField>

Adhere to these rules to maintain account health.
